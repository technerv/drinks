An Interactive GIS Web Mapping Tool For Management Of Alcohol Selling Entities.

Features
  • Monitoring Module: The availability of an interactive web mapping tool will enable agencies to monitor and analyze the profile of these alcohol joints.
  • Administrator Module: This is the backend administration where the administrator is assigned to manipulate data that is viewed on the map.
  • Reports Module: Produce soft and hard copy maps showing location of alcohol selling entities.
  • Location View Module: This tool will enable agencies to monitor and analyze the profile of alcohol joints. Those agencies will know the exact locations of these alcohol joints, so that in case of breach of law they can be punished. It will also enable help provide legal locations during provision of permits and licences to new business owners.
